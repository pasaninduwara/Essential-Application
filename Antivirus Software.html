Antivirus Software

Introduction to Antivirus Software:

Antivirus software is a program that helps protect your computer, phone, or tablet from viruses and other harmful software (called "malware"). It scans your device to find and remove threats, keeping your information safe.

1. Windows Defender (Microsoft Defender)

  • Built into Windows 10 and 11. Automatically protects against viruses and malware.
    Great for: Basic protection with no need to install anything.
    - No download needed. it's already on your Windows computer.
    - Learn more: Click Here

2. Avast Free Antivirus

  • Scans for viruses, blocks harmful websites, and keeps your system safe.
    Great for: Free and easy protection for beginners.

3. Kaspersky Free Antivirus

  • Blocks viruses, phishing attacks, and dangerous downloads
    Great for: Strong protection with a simple design.
