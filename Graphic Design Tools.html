Graphic Design Tools

Introduction to Graphic Design Tools:

Graphic design tools are software programs that help people create images, designs, and layouts for things like posters, websites, logos, and social media posts. These tools make it easier to turn creative ideas into visuals.

Raster (Photo Editing) Tools

  • Adobe Photoshop
    Lets you edit photos, add text, remove objects, and create designs.
    Great for: Photo editing, social media posts, and digital art.
  • GIMP
    Free tool like Photoshop. You can edit images, fix colors, and add effects.
    Great for: People who want a free photo editor.

Vector (Drawing & Logo Design) Tools

  • Adobe Illustrator
    Used to draw shapes, logos, and cartoons that stay sharp at any size.
    Great for: Making logos, icons, and illustrations.
  • CorelDRAW
    A design program for making posters, logos, and page layouts.
    Great for: Professionals who want an all-in-one design tool.

Layout & Publishing Tools

  • Adobe InDesign
    Used to design books, magazines, brochures, and flyers.
    Great for: Laying out pages with text and pictures.
  • Canva
    Easy drag-and-drop tool with free templates. Works online and on mobile.
    Great for: Beginners making social media posts, flyers, or invitations.
